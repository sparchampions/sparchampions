How to Play

Master the battlefield

⚔️
Battle Cards
Your fighters. Each has HP, attack power, and a unique SPAR ability triggered by rolling their number.
Draw 6 per team
✨
Supporter Cards
One-use power boosts. Play at the right moment to turn the tide of battle in your favor.
Draw 3 per team
01🃏
Setup
Shuffle all Battle and Supporter cards thoroughly. Each team draws 6 Battle cards and 3 Supporter cards. Keep your hand secret.
02🎲
First Roll
Both teams roll the die. The team with the highest roll goes first. Teams then alternate turns during the battle.
03⚔️
Choose Your Champion
Each team selects 1 Battle card to send into combat. Remaining cards are kept secret and used in future rounds.
04💥
Taking Turns
On your turn, roll the die. The number rolled equals damage dealt to the opponent's active card. Subtract from their HP.
05💀
Card Defeat
When a card's HP reaches 0, it is discarded into the discard pile. The team must send a new card into combat.
06✨
Supporter Cards
Use Supporter cards as instructed to boost your battle cards. These are single-use — spend them wisely!
✦
